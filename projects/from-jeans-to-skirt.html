Denim Upcycling with NCCFN

This upcycled denim skirt was created in collaboration with NCCFN. This project deals with the effects of the broken clothing industry and addresses the following questions regarding this unsustainable issue: As designers, how should we design for a clothing industry that stimulates us to consume? How can we reuse what surrounds us in a practical way, facing the challenge to use existing materials that has to meet aesthetics, quality and functionality at the same time? Through this project and my stay at NCCFN, I get to know new design and style elements, such as deconstructing and transforming, that combine aesthetics of quality and design without another piece of clothing being newly produced and brought into our textile cycle.
