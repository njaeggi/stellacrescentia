Capsule Wardrobe

This work was created as part of the final class collection. The process starts with extensive research, followed by the planning and development of a concept, but unfolds its full potential during implementation and ends with the final result, a unique class collection. Our theme “Capsule Wardrobe” serves as the inspiration and starting point for the subsequent collection. The entire process from inspiration to the finished collection was documented in a brochure which is written from my perspective.
