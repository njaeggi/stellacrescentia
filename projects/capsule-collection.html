CAPSULE COLLECTION

In the final year of my education, our graduating class had the opportunity to design our own collection concept together. The process began modestly with extensive research, followed by the planning and development of a concept. It then unfolded its full potential during implementation and culminated in an impressive final result – a unique collection. Our theme, “Capsule Wardrobe,” served as both inspiration and the starting point for the final collection. The entire process, from initial inspiration to the finished collection, was documented in a magazine.
