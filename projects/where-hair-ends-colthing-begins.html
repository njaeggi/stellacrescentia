WHERE HAIR ENDS AND CLOTHING BEGINS

Hair, for us an everyday familiar substance based on keratin. I play with the dimension of human hair, which at first seems limited, but now merges into clothing in a new context and no longer has clear boundaries. What happens when roles are mixed up, swapped and changed? A textile surface becomes hair, hair becomes textile. Both materials flow into each other and combine to form a new surface, the hairline is boundless.

With my collection of new interpreted materials and silhouettes, I want to call attention to the problem of the fur industry, why don't we use our own hair in the future?
Let yourself be confused, let yourself be inspired!
